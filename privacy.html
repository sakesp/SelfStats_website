SelfStats Privacy Policy

Last updated June 6, 2026

Personal data

SelfStats runs on your device and does not collect your personal data. The app does not require an account, does not include a social feed, and does not use remote tracking.

App data

The stats, themes, entries, and settings you create in SelfStats are stored on your device. You can choose to copy stats into a spreadsheet, export CSV files, or back up app data as JSON.

Exports and backups

When you export, copy, or back up data, you control where that file or copied text goes. SelfStats does not send those exports to a SelfStats account or social service.

Contact

For privacy questions about SelfStats, email support@selfstats.app.
