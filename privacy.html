SelfStats Privacy Policy

Last updated June 6, 2026

Personal data

SelfStats runs on your device and does not collect your personal data. The app does not require an account, does not contact a SelfStats server, does not include ads, and does not use remote tracking, analytics, or crash reporting.

App data

The stats, themes, entries, and settings you create in SelfStats are stored on your device. SelfStats does not use iCloud directly. You can choose to copy stats, export CSV files, or back up app data as JSON.

Exports and backups

When you export, copy, or back up data, you control where that file or copied text goes. SelfStats does not send those exports to a SelfStats account, backend, or social service. If you choose to share an export with another app or service, that destination may handle the data according to its own privacy policy.

Contact

For privacy questions about SelfStats, email support@selfstats.app. If you contact support, your email address and message will be used only to respond to your request.
