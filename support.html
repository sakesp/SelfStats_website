SelfStats Support

Help for SelfStats, a simple iPhone app for keeping score of the things you want to do more often.

Getting help

If you need help with SelfStats, use the support contact listed with the app in the App Store. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Common tasks

  • Create counting, number-entry, or checkbox stats for anything you want to track.
  • Group stats into themes so different parts of your life stay easy to find.
  • Open a stat detail page to review history, rates, streaks, and adjustment options.
  • Copy stats to a spreadsheet, export CSV files, or back up app data as JSON.
